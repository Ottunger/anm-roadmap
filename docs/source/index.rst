Welcome to ANote Music's roadmap!
===================================

**ANote Music** is the European exchange for music rights, allowing anyone to tap into this new asset class while supporting his/her
favorite artists.
ANote Music tries to get the best of both worlds: *crypto* that allows people to participate
more anonymously, to interact without central administration; but also *API platform* to serve people who still
prefer traditional banking means.

Check out the :doc:`api` section for further information on the API
while :doc:`roadmap` will give you an overview of our current goals.

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
